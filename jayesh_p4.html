Welcome to the Homepage

Priyadarshini J.L. College of Engineering (PJLCE) is an autonomous institution located in Nagpur, Maharashtra, affiliated with Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University. Established in 2008, the college has earned several prestigious certifications, including ISO 9001:2015 for Quality Management, ISO 14001:2015 for Environmental Management, and ISO 50001:2018 for Energy Management. Additionally, it holds an 'A' grade accreditation from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).
